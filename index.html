We aim to help farmers reduce the risk of developing diseases as a result of overexposure to pesticides and fertilizers.

Regulating the use of farm chemicals

Often due to lack of knowledge, farmers end up using inadequate or excess amount of fertilizers or pesticides

This is where our drones come in

Our drones, operated by trained operators will distribute pesticides and fertilizers evenly over the entire land area. This can be provided as a service to the farmers at a minimal rate with the help of government subsidy..
